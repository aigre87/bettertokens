BetterTokens Team
Alexander Ivanov
Founder and Chair of Tokenization Standards Association

Alexander is CEO of Waves Platform and Vostok.
One of the most prominent, respected and influential people in the blockchain space, Alexander has led his company and its community towards strong growth, international recognition, and financial rewards for the last two years.
The Waves Platform is now a global public blockchain platform with a market cap of $1.7 billion as of 19 December 2017.

Artem Tolkachev
Co-founder and Board Member for Tokenization Standards Association

A well-known expert in the field of blockchain and cryptocurrencies, Artem has been involved with legal support for crypto-related projects since 2013.

In 2016, Artem founded and headed Deloitte Blockchain Lab CIS. As part of that initiative, he led a range of innovative projects involving implementation of corporate blockchain solutions, tokenisation of real-world assets, ICO support and development of legislation on cryptocurrencies and blockchain.

Georgiy Gukasyan
Secretary of Tokenization Standards Association

For the last five years, Georgiy has provided consulting services to the financial services sector.

Georgiy has hands-on knowledge of the fintech and crypto industries, having taken part in a number of global projects focused on ICO support, tokenisation of assets and structuring of crypto exchange operations.

Georgiy worked at Deloitte CIS and holds a law degree from Madrid Complutense University in Spain.
